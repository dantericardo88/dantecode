What the picture means

The animation is meant to answer the honest question quickly: are we in the conversation, and where exactly do we still lose?

Where we are strong Verification-first generation, safety posture, local model routing, and proof-oriented engineering discipline are real advantages.
Where the frontier still beats us Hard-task finishing, ambiguity handling, retrieval under pressure, and premium editor/runtime polish still separate us from the top cluster.
What is true today DanteCode looks like the strongest open-source coding product in this set, but still sits just below the closed-source frontier cluster on pure coding functionality.
Fast read We are not pretending parity. We are showing that we are close enough to learn aggressively, while still being honest about the remaining gap.
